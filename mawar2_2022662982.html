    We promote the basic design concepts

    1. Contrast

      Size , color , weight , opacity , etc

      Creates a hierarchy of importance

    2. Repetition

      Repeat similar element (colors , shapes , sizes , etc) to make the design cohesive and balanced

    3. Alignment

      All elements should have locational relationship to each other (lined-up)

      Directs the flow and directions of the user's focus

    4. Proximity

      Groups similar components for clearer definition

      "Blocks" of ideas make a more interesting layout and will more likely hold the user's attention
